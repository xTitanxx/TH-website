Three bedrooms. Two living spaces. A proper kitchen, recently redone. Three full bathrooms and a half.

The light moves across the top two floors all day — two balconies on each floor, one facing each direction, so there is always somewhere in the sun and somewhere in the shade. Open doors at each end and air crosses the house.

Furniture and textiles are layered slowly, mostly by hand, mostly found. Nothing is loud. Everything fits.

Casa Indigo is spread over two floors — there are stairs throughout. If stairs aren't for you, Studio Indigo downstairs is step-free.
