Three floors, one building, three ways to stay.

At street level: Valerie's shop, and next door Studio Indigo — a self-contained, step-free retreat. Above them, Casa Indigo spreads across the top two floors — three bedrooms, two living spaces, four balconies looking in every direction.

The street is for walking, not driving. You hear footsteps, voices drifting out of the shop, birds on the balconies, church bells. Not much else.
